Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Web developing is obviously held to standards that when new forms of user interface concepts are developed that the user should come to expect comes that almost as way of web development law the interface should be included into the website, or risk of lost traffic may occur when users find that another site has developed a way to implement it better. I feel this is seen in social media over the past few years as their seems to be a focus to add other platforms options into everything to make sure users have all options for spreading their information to customers or friends.
